Most training apps pick a side. They're either a logbook for solo lifters or a
clipboard for coaches. FitApp is built for the relationship between the two.

## One plan, two views

A coach builds a structured program — days, exercises, sets, and reps — and shares
it with an athlete through a simple invite code. The athlete follows that exact
plan, logs every set as they go, and the coach sees the history without asking for
a screenshot.

- **Athletes** get structured programs, per-set tracking, a calendar of scheduled
  and completed workouts, and inline form videos beside each exercise.
- **Coaches** get an overview of every connected athlete, program sharing, and
  progress monitoring across sessions.

## Built in the open

FitApp is an open-source, protocol-style project — contributors earn from the
revenue pool. It's built with Flutter and Firebase and runs on web, iOS, and
Android from a single codebase.
