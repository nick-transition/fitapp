FitApp ("we", "us") is a fitness coaching platform. This policy explains what we
collect and why.

## Information we collect

- **Account information** — your name, email address, and authentication details
  (via Google or email/password).
- **Training data** — the programs, workouts, and per-set session logs you create
  or that a coach shares with you.
- **Usage data** — basic, aggregated information about how the app is used, to keep
  it reliable.

## How we use information

We use your information to provide the service: syncing your training data across
devices, connecting athletes and coaches, and processing subscription payments
through our payment provider (Stripe).

## Data storage

Your data is stored using Google Firebase. Payments are processed by Stripe; we do
not store full payment card details on our servers.

## Your choices

You can access and update your account information in the app. To request deletion
of your account and associated data, contact us at the address below.

## Contact

Questions about this policy? Email **privacy@fitapp.com**.
